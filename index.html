About

I am a graduate student at The University of Texas in Austin working under the guidance of and in collaboration with Dr. Hans Hofmann. I study the neuromolecular basis of social behavior and am involved in several initiatives to enhance training in the life sciences.

Professional Interests

• My research aims to better characterize variability in neuromolecular mechanisms regulating diverse behaviors, especially social behaviors • My educational efforts aim to transform graduate training in computational approaches to biological questions • My outreach activities are tailored to promote the sharing of ideas across typical disciplinary boundaries
